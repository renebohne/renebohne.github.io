About this site

This blog is a collection of my projects. I write about things that I did in the past and new ideas that I am currently working on.

About Me

My name is René Bohne, I am a maker and a computer scientist.

For the past 6 years, I worked at the Media Computing Group of RWTH Aachen University.
I was the manager of FabLab RWTH Aachen, Germany's first Fab Lab, founded 2009.
I was the Business Unit Manager for German/English markets at Elektor International Media.

Now, I am the Startup Program Manager at Telefónica Germany NEXT GmbH, a free author and consultant, and about to found a company called WEAR LEDS!

I will finish my PhD in computer science about Personal Fabrication soon.
